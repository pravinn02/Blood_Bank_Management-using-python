About LifeStream Blood Bank

Ensuring a safe and steady blood supply is crucial for saving lives. At Bhavisya Blood Bank, we are committed to maintaining a robust blood donation system that plays a vital role in healthcare.

Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a selfless act that helps save lives during emergencies, surgeries, and various medical treatments.

Blood Types and Compatibility

Understanding the different blood types and their compatibility is crucial for matching donors with recipients and ensuring successful transfusions.

Importance of Regular Blood Drives

Organizing regular blood drives is essential for maintaining an ample blood supply, especially during times of increased demand or emergencies.
